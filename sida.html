A Swedish Legacy of Purity & Science

What started as exports to Slovenia has grown into a global presence across 20+ countries on every continent, with branch offices worldwide. Our headquarters and production facility remain in Sweden, where we maintain pharmaceutical-grade standards that exceed both Swedish and EU requirements.

Environmentally Protected Waters

Our Own Arctic Ocean Territory

We hold exclusive, environmentally protected harvest rights in the pristine Arctic waters surrounding Iceland and Greenland — the only regions on Earth still free from industrial pollution, heavy shipping, and agricultural runoff.

Our seaweed grows wild in nutrient-dense glacial waters, absorbing trace minerals from ancient volcanic bedrock and benefiting from 24-hour summer sunlight. Every harvest is done by hand or small fishing boats (never industrial trawlers) to preserve the delicate marine ecosystem.
