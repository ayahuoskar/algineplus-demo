How to Take Algine Plus

Follow this proven protocol for best results. Start slow, build up, and feel the difference.

1
Day 1: Start with 2 tablets
Take in the morning with water. This allows your body to adjust to the natural iodine and nutrient density.
2
Days 2-5: Add 1 tablet per day
Increase gradually: 3 tablets on day 2, 4 on day 3, until you reach 5 tablets on day 5.
3
Maintenance: 5 tablets every morning
This is your daily dose. Contains 1500mg seaweed blend, 200µg iodine, 163mg calcium, and 500µg CoQ10.
4
Athletes: 1 tablet per 10kg body weight
If you train hard (CrossFit, marathon, weightlifting), you can take up to 1 tablet per 10kg. Example: 80kg person = 8 tablets/day.
⚠️ Important: Do NOT take Algine Plus close to bedtime. The natural energy boost from spirulina and CoQ10 can keep you awake. Always take in the morning!
